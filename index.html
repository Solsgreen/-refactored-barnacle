We're Solsgreen Farms

About Us

It all starts with the plants, cultivated using all natural ingredients and organic growing conditions.

Solsgreen is multifacted and highly dynamic OLCC licensed retail cannabis producer, located in southern Oregon. Aside to a commitment to delivering customers high purity and high quality cannabis products, Solsgreen is also a direct importer of extraction equipment used for the production of essential cannabis oils. We acknowledge the best product can only be obtained with the best ingredients and this drives our commitment to only produce and process cannabis which has been cultivated organically and pesticide free.

Solsgreen Cannabis offers organically grown buds, pre-rolled joints, and concentrates for the vaporizer cartirgde market. Solsgreen Processing offers ethanol extraction, supercrtical fluid extraction, distillates, terpene extracts, topical creme, and small scale artisan custom extracts.

Our Process

We use natural sources for cultiavtion to ensure the most favorable wavelengths of light are delivered to our plants.

We only get what we put into our product, which is why our soil is organic and pesticide free.
